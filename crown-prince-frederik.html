Biography

Crown Prince Frederik was born in Copenhagen, Denmark on May 26, 1968. Became known as the Crown Prince of Denmark and heir to the throne as the eldest son of Queen Margrethe II and Henrik, the Prince Consort. His mother and Queen Elizabeth II became two of the longest-reigning queens in history. He was named Frederik after his maternal grandfather, King Frederick IX as part of Danish Royal tradition.
