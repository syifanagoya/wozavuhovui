Ben Whishaw Net Worth

January 2024 · 1 minute read

Ben Whishaw net worth:

$2 million

Ben Whishaw is an English actor. His acting career started in the late ’90s with his debut in the independent film The Trench. His starring role in the movie My Brother Tom got him a British Independent Film Award for Most Promising Newcomer. He went on to portray Keith Richards in the film Stoned. He gained wider recognition for playing the lead in the film Perfume: The Story of a Murderer. Subsequently, he starred in the film Brideshead Revisited and portrayed John Keats in Bright Star. In the 2010s, he starred in Skyfall, Cloud Atlas, The Lobster, In the Heart of the Sea, and A Hologram for the King.
