Enji Night was Born 32 Years Ago and was Raised Partly in Russia and Hungary

Enji Night was born Anna Redei on the 30th of September 1991 in Budapest, Hungary. Her father is half German and half Hungarian while her mother is half Russian and half Mongolian. However, she has black siblings from her father’s side.

The 32 year old lady grew up in both Russia and Hungary. She spent her early years with her grandfather in Russia before going back to Hungary to begin elementary school. As a result, she learned how to speak Russian early on, but she started to forget it slowly since she had abandoned it and was now speaking Hungarian.

The model has chosen to reveal this much about her childhood while she has kept further details about her family and personal life private. The names of her parents and siblings (if any) are not known. She has also mentioned that she was lonely and shy as a kid and her mum made her wear her hair short until she turned thirteen. This made many people, even her neighbors, think that she was a boy.

Regarding her educational journey, Anna has not revealed the schools she attended from high school to college. Perhaps, she has chosen to focus more on her modeling and Cosplaying career.

The Hungarian Busojaras Festival Spurred Enji Night’s Interest in Costume Designing

Some people know, or at least have an idea of what they would love to be in the future as kids, while others figure things out along the way. Enji Night belongs to the former group. She developed an interest in her current profession at an early age and her journey through the industry has been an interesting one.

As mentioned earlier, Anna picked interest in fashion and costumes from a young age. Her interest was fueled by the traditions of her home country, Hungary which holds an elaborate festival for costume display.

In Hungary, especially Mohac town, the people have a long-standing traditional festival called “Busojaras” or “The Hungarian Festival That Scares Winter Away”. During the festival, people dress in different scary costumes which they believe will scare away winter and welcome spring and its good spirits. The festival always lasts for six days, and in recent times, the locals practice it every last week of February.

Aside from the tradition of her people, another thing that greatly influenced her career choice was her love for animation series. Enji revealed in an interview that she started watching a lot of anime at age 13. Her new hobby soon developed into a love for manga, and online gaming. Over time, she fell in love with the anime characters and the way they dressed and she wanted to look like them.

Anna Redei nursed her passion for a long time until she decided to take advantage of social media. She started on Instagram as a fashion model. She regularly uploaded irresistible lifestyle photos and videos. Gradually, she started to garner followers on the platform. Who wouldn’t want to see more attractive photos and videos of a pretty lady with a perfect hourglass shape?

Soon, Night introduced her cosplay lifestyle to her Instagram page and launched her career fully. As you may have expected, her rising fan base grew even faster and she started to receive more attention across the globe. In less than no time, she was traveling to different places, including Tunisia and the Philippines for cosplay photoshoots, events, and activities.
